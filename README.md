How do I approach designing software?

I approach designing software by first understanding the problem and breaking it into smaller, manageable components. For this project, I analyzed the reference image and identified the primary structural elements of the scene, such as the lighthouse, surrounding buildings, terrain, and rocks. From there, I designed the scene using reusable primitives and transformations to keep the system modular and adaptable.


What new design skills have your work on the project helped you to craft?

This project helped me improve my ability to design complex scenes using basic geometry and transformations, rather than relying on imported models. I also strengthened my skills in visual composition, spatial layout, and organizing objects so that the scene feels balanced, readable, and purposeful.


What design process did you follow for your project work?

I followed an iterative design process. I began with simple shapes to establish scale and placement, then progressively added textures, lighting, materials, and interaction. Each milestone allowed me to refine proportions, visual realism, and user navigation based on testing and feedback.


How could tactics from your design approach be applied in future work?

Breaking complex problems into reusable components and iterating frequently applies to any software project. This approach makes systems easier to maintain, debug, and extend while supporting future enhancements.


How do I approach developing programs?

I focus on writing modular, readable code that clearly separates responsibilities. I use helper functions, descriptive naming, and consistent formatting to ensure the code is easy to follow and maintain.


What new development strategies did you use while working on your 3D scene?

I relied heavily on reusable transformation functions, centralized material and texture management, and uniform shader updates. These strategies reduced redundancy and improved the clarity and efficiency of the rendering pipeline.


How did iteration factor into your development?

Iteration was essential throughout development. Each milestone allowed me to test functionality, improve visuals, and refine interaction. I frequently revisited earlier decisions to improve performance, readability, or visual quality.


How has your approach to developing code evolved throughout the milestones that led you to the project’s completion?

Over time, my approach shifted from experimenting with individual features to building a cohesive and well-structured system. The final project reflects more deliberate planning, cleaner architecture, and stronger confidence in debugging and optimization.


How can computer science help me in reaching my goals?

Computer science provides problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and technical knowledge that can be applied across many fields. These skills support both creative projects and professional software development goals.
